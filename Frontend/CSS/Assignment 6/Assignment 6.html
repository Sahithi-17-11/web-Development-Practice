The history of coffee dates back to the 15th century, and possibly earlier. As it's thought that coffee originated in Ethiopia, it's also believed it made its way north across the red sea into Yemen in the 15th Century. It then started to be grown in the Yemeni district of Arabia, and by the 16th century it was known in Persia, Egypt, Syria, and Turkey.

According to legend, a goat herder named Kaldi was the first person who discovered coffee beans and their benefits.
